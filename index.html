About me

I am now a third year graduate student in Mathematics Department at The University of Texas at Austin, advised by Prof. Thaleia Zariphopoulou. My main research studies financial mathematics, stochastic optimization and data-driven decision making under uncertainty. In particular, I develop robust and computationally efficient methodology with strong performance guarantees for learning and decision-making problems involving contextual/observational data, arising in the area of machine learning, risk management and revenue management.

I received my bachelor degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics (Honors Science Group, 珠峰计划) at Xi’an Jiaotong University, where I was also a student of the Honors Youth Program (少年班) before my undergraduate study.
