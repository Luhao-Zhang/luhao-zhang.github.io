About me

I am now a fourth year graduate student in Mathematics Department at The University of Texas at Austin, advised by Prof. Thaleia Zariphopoulou.

My research interests include:

  • Financial mathematics, stochastic optimization. My recent research includes applying stochastic control techniques to explore the optimal way to acquire information dynamically to facilitate decision-making in portfolio management and optimal trading.
  • Data-driven decision making under uncertainty. In particular, I develop robust and computationally efficient methodology with strong performance guarantees for learning and decision-making problems involving contextual/observational data, arising in the area of machine learning, risk management and revenue management.

I received my bachelor degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics (Honors Science Group, 珠峰计划) at Xi’an Jiaotong University, where I was also a student of the Speical Class for the Gifted Youth Program (少年班) before my undergraduate study.
